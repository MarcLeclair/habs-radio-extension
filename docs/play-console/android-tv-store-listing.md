# Store Listing Draft - Android TV

## Short Description

Play synchronized hockey radio audio on Android TV while watching another video app.

## Full Description

Puck Radio Sync TV plays a radio stream directly on your Android TV and exposes simple local-network controls for the Puck Radio Remote phone app. Open the TV app, start playback, then use the phone remote to adjust delay and volume so the radio call lines up with your video stream.

The app keeps radio playback under your control while you use compatible video apps. Audio behavior can still vary depending on the video app and device.

The app does not use accounts, analytics, ads, or tracking.
