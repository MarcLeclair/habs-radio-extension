# Store Listing Draft - Android Phone Remote

## Short Description

Control Puck Radio Sync on Android TV from your phone.

## Full Description

Puck Radio Remote finds the Puck Radio Sync TV app on your local Wi-Fi and gives you quick controls for playback, sync delay, and volume. Use it to line up the radio call with the video stream playing on your TV.

The app does not use accounts, analytics, ads, or tracking.
